Welcome to DEKRA Institute of Learning (IOL)

At DEKRA IOL, we are committed to fostering growth and continuous improvement for both our institution and our learners. With nearly a century of experience, DEKRA has established itself as a global leader in non-destructive testing (NDT), inspection, certification, and consulting services. Our mission is to be the global partner for a safe world, ensuring safety, quality, and environmental protection in all aspects of life.

Our History and Legacy

Founded almost 100 years ago, DEKRA has a rich history of innovation and excellence. Our journey began with a focus on vehicle inspections and has since expanded to encompass a wide range of services across various industries. Today, DEKRA operates in over 50 countries, providing expert solutions that meet the highest standards of safety and quality.

Our Services

DEKRA IOL offers a comprehensive suite of training and educational programs designed to equip individuals with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in their careers. Our offerings include:

  • Blended Learning: Flexible alternatives to traditional classroom-based education, combining online and in-person training.
  • Academic Support: Assistance through various channels, including telephone, email, online discussions, and video downloads.
  • Industry Training: Accredited by the Quality Council for Trades and Occupations (QCTO), we provide HSE-based and ISO training services across industries, including government institutions and the commercial sector.

Our Commitment to Excellence

We pride ourselves on our dedication to safety, quality, and innovation. Our team of skilled educators, assessors, and moderators are passionate about delivering top-notch training that not only meets but exceeds industry standards. DEKRA IOL is also proud to be a BB-BEE Level 1 company, ensuring that our training programs contribute to the development and empowerment of our community.

Our Vision

Aligned with our parent company's Vision 2025, DEKRA IOL aims to be the global partner for a safe world. We strive to achieve this through our extensive expertise, wide-ranging services, and passionate commitment to long-term safety, quality, and environmental protection.
