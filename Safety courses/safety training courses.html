About DEKRA

At DEKRA, we provide a variety of health and safety training courses aimed at improving workplace safety and compliance. Our comprehensive training programs are designed to meet the highest standards and are accredited by several reputable organizations, ensuring that our clients receive top-quality education and support.

Our Accreditations

  • ISO 9001: DEKRA is certified under ISO 9001, which ensures that our quality management systems are robust and effective.
  • ISO 14001: This certification highlights our commitment to environmental management standards.
  • ISO 45001: DEKRA is accredited for occupational health and safety management systems, ensuring that our training programs meet international safety standards.
  • ISO 9712: Our NDT (Non-Destructive Testing) training programs are certified under ISO 9712, ensuring the highest level of competence and reliability in our inspection services.
  • RD-0034: We are in the process of meeting the RD-0034 nuclear safety compliance standard, which will make us one of the few companies certified to this Level 2 supplier qualification in South Africa.

Our Training Programs

We offer a wide range of training programs, including:

  • Occupational Health and Safety Awareness Training: Designed to educate employees on the importance of workplace safety and how to identify and mitigate potential hazards.
  • ISO Consulting Services: Our experts provide guidance on achieving and maintaining ISO certifications, helping organizations improve their management systems and compliance.
  • NDT Training: Our Non-Destructive Testing courses cover various methods and techniques, ensuring that personnel are well-equipped to perform inspections and maintain safety standards.

With 96 years of expertise, DEKRA remains at the forefront of innovation and technology, continuously enhancing our training programs to meet the evolving needs of our clients. Our dedicated team, including our Services Division representative for Europe based in South Africa, ensures that we stay updated with the latest advancements and share this knowledge with our clients, providing a significant strategic advantage.

Imagine what the next 100 years will bring as we continue to lead in safety and compliance training!
