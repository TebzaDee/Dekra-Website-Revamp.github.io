VISION ZERO Campaign

At DEKRA Institute of Learning, we are committed to supporting the VISION ZERO initiative by Saiosh. This global campaign aims to reduce workplace accidents and occupational health issues to zero. Our training programs are designed with the VISION ZERO principles in mind, providing learners with the knowledge and tools to promote a safe and healthy working environment.

The 7 Golden Rules for VISION ZERO

  • 1. Take Leadership: Demonstrate commitment to health and safety.
  • 2. Identify Hazards: Evaluate and control risks proactively.
  • 3. Define Targets: Set clear goals for safety improvements.
  • 4. Ensure a Safe and Healthy System: Design robust management systems.
  • 5. Use Safe Technology: Implement reliable and safe technologies.
  • 6. Improve Qualifications: Train employees to recognize risks.
  • 7. Invest in People: Foster a culture of safety through continuous investment in employees' well-being.

We encourage all companies to adopt the VISION ZERO principles to ensure a safer workplace for everyone.
